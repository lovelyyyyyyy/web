About Us

TrainFresher was established to enable students and freshers to take charge of their careers. It aims to train young talent, make them industry-ready, and help them kick start their career with a decent job. The organization focuses on building essential technical skills, provides exposure by letting students work on real projects, and offers industrial experience. Incepted by Pradep Raney, a tech guru who has trained, consulted, and built technological infrastructures for organizations across the world, TrainFresher is now helping freshers build the infrastructure for their dreams. The corporate world is now focusing vehemently on the Future of Work. With TrainFresher, we ensure you are riding the wave at the lead and are adapting seamlessly to the change. The courses you undergo with us will certify you with official Microsoft and/or Azure credentials. They will equip you with the technology that the industry demands right now and for the future. Furthermore, TrainFresher goes a step beyond training and also mentors you through your tech career. We understand what drives your decisions, what motivates you to get out of bed each morning, and what could be the best avenue for your success. If you are ready to build a pedestal for your dreams and take the first step to soar high, get in touch with us and explore the immense possibilities that await you.
